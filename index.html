About Aerolux

Our story, values, and commitment to excellence in air travel

Redefining Air Travel Since 2010

Aerolux was founded with a simple mission: to elevate the air travel experience through exceptional service, innovative technology, and uncompromising comfort.

What began as a small regional airline has grown into an international leader in premium air travel, serving over 50 destinations across 6 continents.

Our commitment to sustainability, customer satisfaction, and operational excellence has earned us numerous industry awards and the loyalty of millions of passengers.
